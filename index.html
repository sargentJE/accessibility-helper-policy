Privacy Policy for MyVision Accessibility Helper

Last updated: 2 May 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Accessibility Helper Chrome Extension (“Extension”), developed by MyVision Oxfordshire, collects, uses, and safeguards your data. The Extension exists to gather accessibility feedback and foster digital inclusion.

What Information We Collect

  • Website URLs: Pages you review.
  • Form Responses: Your answers to accessibility questions.
  • Browser Info: Basic environment details (e.g. browser version).
  • Settings Preferences: Theme, font-size, contrast toggles.
  • Email & Auth Token: Used solely for sign-in to MyVision’s backend.

How We Use Your Information

  • Aggregate feedback to identify common accessibility issues.
  • Improve the Extension’s functionality and user experience.
  • Conduct research on accessibility barriers.

Data Storage & Security

  • All communication takes place over secure HTTPS.
  • Form data is encrypted during transit.
  • Data is stored on secure servers with strict access controls.
  • We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes above.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share personal data except:

  • When required by law.
  • With your explicit consent.
  • In anonymised, aggregated form.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Access or export your data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of future data collection.

Email us at accessibility@myvision.org.uk to exercise these rights.

Data Minimisation Commitment

We collect only data strictly necessary for the Extension’s purpose.

Limited Use Statement

Use of information obtained from Google APIs complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Changes to This Policy

Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
